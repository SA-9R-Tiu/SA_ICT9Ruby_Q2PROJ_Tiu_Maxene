Icy

Berry

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • Strawberries


Steps:
  1. Mix the condensed milk, cold milk, vanila extract, and salt in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Beat the heavy cream in a big bowl with a mixer until it becomes soft. Fold milk mixture into whipped cream.
  3. Pour into a shallow 2-quart dish, cover, and freeze for 4 hours, stirring once after 2 hours or when edges start to harden.
  4. Lastly, add the strawberries so you can serve and enjoy.
