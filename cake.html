Berry

Cakey

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Whipped Cream
  • strawberries


Steps:
  1. Whisk the milk, water, eggs, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the flour and butter.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle. Make sure it isn't too thin so we can stack it nicely later!
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
  6. Lastly, Design it with whipped cream and strawberries on top.
