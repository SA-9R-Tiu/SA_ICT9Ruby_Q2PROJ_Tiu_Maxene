About Us!

Welcome to our store!

First, let us start off with the origin of our name 'Berry Tasty Dreams'. Our menu consists of food with berries specifically strawberry. What we mean by tasty dreams is that of course, our food tastes good and usually, dreams are things that we want to happen or something good. So basically, Tasty Dreams are food that tastes so good you think it's just a dream.

Our restaurant started off as a dream now, we made it come true. The recipes at first were tricky to make but now, we made it easier and we want to share it to you!
