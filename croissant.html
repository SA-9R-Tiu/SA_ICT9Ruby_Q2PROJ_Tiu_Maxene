Berry

Croissant

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 cup cornstarch
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanila extract
  • strawberries (as much as you want)
  • croissants


Steps:
  1. Make the Cream for filling: In a saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt. In another bowl add the egg yolks and half and half, whisking until smooth.
  2. Stir in the butter and place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Remove from heat and and vanilla extract until well combined. Transfer the pastry cream to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap (press it directly onto the surface), and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
  3. Assemble the Strawberry Croissants: After making the cream, put the strawberries in the croissant and also put the cream.
  4. Serve and Enjoy: Dust the top with powdered sugar for a touch of sweetness and extra flair. Serve your Strawberry Croissants immediately.
